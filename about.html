Our Story

British Fanzine of the Year 2012 & 2019

First published in 1989 when Manchester United was very different to the corporate beast it has become, United We Stand is an independent voice of Manchester United supporters.

Award-winning and best-selling, we've published over 300 issues — 11 per season — and been named British Fanzine of the Year in 2012 and 2019. UWS continues to be the authentic voice of match-going Reds.

The majority of UWS writers watch Manchester United home and away. They're influential, incisive, irreverent and work for a mag which is proudly independent and funded by sales. Each issue features 44 pages and tells it like it is at MUFC.
