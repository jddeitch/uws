Independent Since 1989

First published in 1989, United We Stand is the authentic voice of Manchester United supporters. Edited by Andy Mitten, a lifelong Red who watches United home and away, UWS delivers honest, informed opinion from those who actually go to the games.

Award-winning and best-selling, with over 300 issues published and named British Fanzine of the Year in 2012 and 2019, UWS is proudly independent — not owned by the club, not beholden to advertisers. Each of the 11 issues per season features 44 pages of analysis, opinion, and the truth about Manchester United.

Influential. Incisive. Irreverent.
